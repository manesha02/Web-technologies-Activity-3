Step's to prepare Kulcha:

  • In a large bowl mix 1 cup maida or all-purpose flour.
  • salt(required amount).
  • 1/2 tea spoon sugar.
  • 1 table spoon oil.
  • 2 table spoon of curd.
  • Required amount of water.
  • Kneed the dough untile it is soft.
  • add 2 table spoon and set it aside for 30min.
  • Make small size balls.
  • Sprinkle some nigella seeds on the ball.
  • With the rolling pin flatten the dough and roll each into a small-sized kulcha.
  • Pre-heat the pan for 5min on medium flame.
  • Cook the kulcha both the sides, add one tea spoon oil and cook till it turns golden brorn.
  • Serve them in the plate.

Required Ingrediants for Panner Butter Masala:

  1. Panner blocks
  2. Butter
  3. oil
  4. Make 10 soaked cashew into paste
  5. tea spoon Fennel seed
  6. 1 Bay leaf
  7. 1 Small piece of Cinnamon
  8. 1 Elaichi
  9. 4 Clove
  10. 2 Table spoon of Kashmir Chilli powder
  11. 1/2 Tea spoon of Termeric powder
  12. 1 Table spoon Coriander powder
  13. 1 Table spoon Garam Masala powder
  14. 2 chopped onions
  15. 4 roughly chopped tomato
  16. 1 Table spoon Fresh Cream

Step's to prepare Panner butter Masala:

  • Heat the saute pan for a min.
  • Add 3 table spoon oil.
  • Add Fennel seed, Cinnamon, Elaichi and Clove.
  • Add onion and cook till golden brown.
  • Add tomato and satue it until it is cooked.
  • Wait until it cools down.
  • Add water and make it into a paste.
  • Heat the saute pan again for a min.
  • Add 2 table spoon oil.
  • Add the paste into the oil.
  • Boil The paste for 5 min.
  • Add Chilli powder, Termeric powder, Coriander powder and Garam Masale powder.
  • Mix and Boil it for another 5min
  • Add Bay leaf, cashew paste, panner and fresh cream
  • Off the stove and close the lid
  • Serve it in a bowl

Serve the kulcha and Panner Butter Masala.
